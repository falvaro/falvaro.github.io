Francisco Alvaro

Researcher. PhD Student.

This is the webpage of Francisco Álvaro, member of the Pattern Recognition and Human Language Technologies (PRHLT) research center at Universitat Politècnica de València since 2009. I am currently developing my PhD focused on mathematical expression recognition. My research interests lie in the area of statistical pattern recognition and their applications to handwriting recognition.
